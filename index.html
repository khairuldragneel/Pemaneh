About Pemaneh

Pemaneh is a celebration of authentic Malaysian baking traditions. Our name, derived from the Malay word meaning "to preserve" or "to cherish," reflects our commitment to keeping traditional recipes alive while creating memorable moments for our customers.

Each cake is handcrafted with care, using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques passed down through generations. We believe that great cakes start with great ingredients and a dedication to quality.
